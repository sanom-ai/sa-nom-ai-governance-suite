# Compliance Templates

These templates are starter profiles for regulated deployments in Thailand.

They are not legal advice, not a certification claim, and not a substitute for internal compliance, legal, procurement, or regulator review.

## Included Templates

- `THAI_BANKING_PROFILE.md`: starter control workbook for Thai financial institutions.
- `THAI_GOVERNMENT_PROFILE.md`: starter control workbook for Thai government and public-sector teams.

## How To Use

1. Copy the relevant template into your internal governance workspace.
2. Replace every placeholder owner, system, environment, and evidence reference.
3. Map the template rows to your own policies, risk register, and control owners.
4. Attach SA-NOM evidence exports, smoke reports, provider probes, and deployment readiness reports.
5. Have legal, compliance, and information-security owners approve the final version.
