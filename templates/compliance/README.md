# Compliance Templates

These templates are starter profiles for regulated deployments in Thailand.

## Boundary Labels

Use the templates with these labels in mind:
- `Starter support`: included here
- `Legal review required`: always applies before production or formal compliance use
- `Regulator filing not included`: these files do not become an official submission pack by themselves

These templates are not legal advice, not a certification claim, and not a substitute for internal compliance, legal, procurement, privacy, or regulator review.

## How To Use

1. Copy the relevant template into your internal governance workspace.
2. Replace every placeholder owner, system, environment, and evidence reference.
3. Map the template rows to your own policies, risk register, standards program, and control owners.
4. Attach SA-NOM evidence exports, smoke reports, provider probes, and deployment readiness reports.
5. Mark every row that still needs counsel, DPO, or compliance-owner validation.
6. Have legal, compliance, privacy, information-security, and business owners approve the final version.

## Safe Reading Rule

These templates are good starter workbooks for internal governance and control planning.

They should not be presented as:
- completed legal advice
- regulator-approved language
- final filing packs
- certification evidence on their own
