# Commercial License & Pricing

SA-NOM AI Governance Suite is open source under AGPL-3.0-only.
The community baseline may be used at no cost for evaluation, development, testing, and self-managed internal deployment.

Commercial engagement is meant for organizations that need more than the public baseline alone. The commercial layer should be read as rollout, support, hardening, packaging, and quote-shaped delivery scope around the product, not as a way to hide core platform capability behind a paywall.

## Commercial Positioning

SA-NOM commercial engagement is high-trust, founder-led, and scope-defined. We help organizations shape governed AI deployment, PT-OSS-informed rollout decisions, and private-first operating confidence. Broader execution may be partner-supported where required.

That means customers are not paying for busywork, daily hand-holding, or a fake large-team promise. They are paying for judgment, operating clarity, rollout discipline, and risk reduction around a real product baseline.

## What Commercial Covers

`Role Private Studio` is a core platform capability. The community baseline already includes the real governed runtime through the `v0.7.9` line: PTAG, Role Private Studio, Human Ask, command surface, Control Room, cases, documents, AI actions, master data, search, assignment, audit and evidence, and private-first runtime confidence tooling.

Commercial scope is for organizations that want one or more of the following on top of that baseline:
- guided evaluation and structured first deployment planning
- founder-led governance and rollout direction
- PT-OSS-informed review, calibration, and risk framing
- environment hardening, regulated delivery preparation, or sovereign deployment shaping
- identity, integration, and packaging work beyond the self-managed community lane
- organization-specific JD, workflow, and role tailoring
- scheduled support posture, review cadence, and quote-defined escalation paths
- partner-supported execution when the environment requires more delivery bandwidth than the core advisory scope

## Pricing (2026 baseline)

All installations remain private-server deployments controlled by the customer organization and can be prepared for air-gapped environments.

The prices below should be read as annual commercial pricing bands or quote-shaped engagement classes. Exact scope still depends on deployment posture, workflow criticality, partner involvement, and support depth.

## Recommended Next Step By Need

- If you only need a real product baseline and can self-manage, stay on the `Community` path.
- If you need help shaping the first live environment or deciding whether SA-NOM is the right fit, start with `Guided Evaluation`.
- If you already have a real workflow and want proof in your own environment, `Paid Pilot` is usually the right first paid path.
- If you have already passed a pilot and want founder-level direction without turning SA-NOM into a daily-service dependency, move into `Strategic Rollout Advisory`.
- If the environment is sovereign, public-sector, or heavily regulated, move into `Enterprise / Sovereign / Gov` scoping only when scope, ownership, and execution posture are clear.

## Recurring Commercial Path

For customers that want continuing guidance without re-buying a full project every time, SA-NOM can be extended through a recurring commercial path such as:
- annual maintenance or advisory in the 20-25% range of the initial paid pilot or rollout engagement
- PT-OSS review cadence
- governance board review
- release confidence review
- private registry or private role-pack maintenance
- partner escalation access when broader execution support is in play

Recurring commercial scope should remain scheduled, bounded, and professional. It should not drift into unlimited on-call support.
