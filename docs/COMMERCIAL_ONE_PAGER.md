# Commercial One-Pager

SA-NOM is a private-first governed AI operating system for real roles, real work, and real oversight.
The open-source baseline is already real.
Commercial engagement exists for organizations that want a tighter path to live deployment, clearer rollout decisions, and higher operating confidence around sensitive or important workflows.

## What The Open-Source Baseline Already Includes

Through the `v0.7.9` line, the public baseline already includes:
- PTAG governance language
- Role Private Studio
- Human Ask
- command surface and Control Room
- cases, documents, and AI actions
- master data, search, and assignment
- audit and evidence posture
- private-first runtime confidence tooling

This is not a teaser edition.
It is a real product baseline that can be evaluated and deployed by self-managed teams.

## What Commercial Adds

Commercial engagement is for organizations that need more than self-management.
The commercial layer is not a feature unlock. It is a high-trust operating path around the product.

Commercial work typically adds one or more of the following:
- founder-led workflow and rollout direction
- PT-OSS-informed judgment on structural risk, authority design, and readiness
- structured first deployment planning
- scoped paid pilot work in the customer's own environment
- recurring advisory after a successful pilot
- partner-supported execution when broader delivery bandwidth is required
- regulated, sovereign, procurement, or hardening support beyond the community lane

## Commercial Doctrine

SA-NOM commercial engagement should be read as:
- high-trust
- founder-led
- scope-defined
- quote-shaped
- partner-supported where required

That means customers are paying for judgment, operating clarity, and risk reduction.
They are not paying for a fake large-team promise, open-ended labor, or unlimited daily support.

## Recommended Engagement Path

| Path | Best fit | Typical output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Community** | Self-managed evaluation, internal learning, small controlled internal use | Documentation-first path and real product baseline |
| **Guided Evaluation** | Serious evaluator who wants clarity before committing to a pilot | Structured sessions, workflow framing, deployment recommendation, written next-step guidance |
| **Paid Pilot** | Real workflow, real owner, real environment, real 30-90 day proof window | One workflow validated in the customer's environment with closeout recommendation |
| **Strategic Rollout Advisory** | Customer has passed a pilot and wants continuing founder-level direction | Monthly or quarterly review cadence, rollout sign-off, readiness and escalation review |
| **Enterprise / Sovereign / Gov** | Regulated or sovereign path with clear ownership and broader delivery expectations | Quote-defined commercial program, usually with partner-supported execution |

See [COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md](COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md) for pricing bands and [COMMERCIAL_OFFER_PACKS.md](COMMERCIAL_OFFER_PACKS.md) for practical packaging shapes.

## Best-Fit Organizations

SA-NOM commercial engagement is usually a strong fit when:
- there is a real workflow owner and a real operational problem
- private deployment, auditability, or governed escalation matter
- leadership wants AI to participate in real work, not just chat
- the customer wants a serious first deployment path, not a loose proof of concept
- the organization values a small number of high-quality decisions more than a large amount of generic consulting activity

## Not The Right Fit Yet

A commercial path is usually too early when:
- there is no owner, sponsor, or decision path
- the workflow is still vague
- the customer wants unlimited support on a small budget
- the customer expects a full implementation team by default
- there is no real deployment intent and only general curiosity

## Suggested Next Step

Use this simple rule:
- if the customer can self-manage, stay on `Community`
- if they need help deciding the first move, start with `Guided Evaluation`
- if they need proof in their own environment, move to `Paid Pilot`
- if the pilot is already successful and they want continuing direction, move to `Strategic Rollout Advisory`
- if the environment is sovereign, public-sector, or highly regulated, use a selective `Enterprise / Sovereign / Gov` quote path
