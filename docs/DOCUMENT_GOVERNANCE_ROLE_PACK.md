# Document Governance Role Pack

This guide introduces a public-safe role pack for SA-NOM's Governed Document Center.

The purpose of this pack is to show how AI can operate inside a controlled document-governance role rather than acting like a loose document chatbot.

## What This Role Pack Is

This role pack represents a `Document Governance Coordination Lead`.

It is designed for organizations that want AI to help with:
- controlled document drafting
- document-class assignment
- review and approval routing
- numbering and metadata preparation
- Human Ask reporting on document posture
- retention and record-governance preparation

The role is governance-oriented, not autonomous final authority.

## Why This Role Matters

Many organizations do not fail because they lack documents.
They fail because documents are inconsistent, approval routing is unclear, superseded versions are hard to track, and retained records are managed informally.

This role pack is meant to reduce that routine governance burden.

## What The Role Should Do

Inside approved boundaries, the role should be able to:
- prepare governed draft structures from approved templates
- assign the correct document class
- prepare numbering and metadata according to rule
- route work to the right reviewer or approver path
- identify missing metadata, missing linked forms, or missing authorities
- summarize document posture through Human Ask
- prepare retention and archive-state information after release

This is real operational work, not only passive lookup.

## What The Role Should Not Do

This role should not autonomously:
- approve controlled release
- waive required review or approval steps
- publish high-impact policies without human confirmation
- override retention rules
- dispose retained records
- expose restricted draft content outside authority boundaries

This keeps the role aligned with SA-NOM's core model of bounded AI work.

## Typical Inputs

This role can work from inputs such as:
- draft policy text
- procedure revision requests
- work-instruction change requests
- linked form references
- document owner and approver assignments
- release-scope information
- retention rules or legal-hold indicators

## Typical Outputs

Typical outputs can include:
- draft controlled document package
- metadata completeness check
- approval-routing summary
- superseded-version link summary
- Human Ask status response
- retained-record preparation note
- meeting-ready document posture summary

## Human Ask Fit

This role pack works especially well with Human Ask.

People should be able to ask things such as:
- which documents are waiting for approval
- which version is active
- which procedures are missing linked forms
- which superseded versions are still retained
- which documents are due for review

That makes the role useful in day-to-day governance work, not only during setup.

## Example Organizational Uses

Examples include:
- ISO-style document control in manufacturing
- policy and procedure governance in corporate support functions
- controlled forms and records governance in audit-heavy environments
- public-sector document accountability with formal approval chains

## Safe Public Claim

The `Document Governance Coordination Lead` is a public-safe SA-NOM role pack that shows how AI can perform routine governed document work inside approved boundaries while humans confirm release, exception, waiver, and higher-risk records-governance decisions.
