# Code of Conduct

We want SA-NOM to be a safe and respectful project for maintainers, users, and contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of expected behavior:
- be respectful in technical disagreement
- focus feedback on code, behavior, and evidence
- assume good intent while staying clear and direct
- help keep the project accessible to newcomers

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- discriminatory language or personal attacks
- publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith disruption of technical discussion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community or the safety of contributors.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact the maintainers at sanomaiarch@gmail.com.
